## What is this?

This page is an **MDX** file. Behin the scenes, it's just Markdown, but I can import and use components directly.

### 1. Alert Boxes (Callouts)

I can drop in alert boxes without writing complex HTML divs every time:

<Callout type="tip" title="Did you know?">
	MDX allows you to use your existing Astro, React, Vue, or Svelte components inside your markdown content!
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning">
	**Warning:** This is extremely powerful. You can even embed full applications here.
</Callout>

### 2. Custom Buttons

Here is a button component used directly in the text flow:

<p class="not-prose py-4">
	<Button href="/garden/" icon="fa-leaf">Go to Garden</Button>
	<Button variant="secondary" href="/" icon="fa-home">Back Home</Button>
</p>

### 3. Embedding Interactive Scenes

I can even embed the **Particle Animation** component right here in the middle of the article:

<div class="relative h-64 w-full border border-white/10 rounded-xl overflow-hidden my-8 not-prose">
	<NeuroBackground />
	<div class="absolute inset-0 flex items-center justify-center pointer-events-none">
		<span class="text-2xl font-bold text-white bg-black/50 px-4 py-2 rounded">Live Canvas Component</span>
	</div>
</div>

This changes everything. The "Lab" isn't separate from the "Article". The article *is* the Lab.
